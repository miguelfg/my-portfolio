# <a href="#" class="u-url">Automatic rebuilds</a>

Nikola can rebuild your site and reload your browser automatically when
you are working on your site.

To use that mode, run `nikola auto `<span class="pre">`-b`</span> and
edit your site. Your browser will open. You can now edit your posts,
create new ones, or add new content. And your site will rebuild in the
background, without any intervention.
