Automatic rebuilds

Nikola can rebuild your site and reload your browser automatically when you are working on your site.

To use that mode, run nikola auto -b and edit your site. Your browser will open. You can now edit your posts, create new ones, or add new content. And your site will rebuild in the background, without any intervention.
